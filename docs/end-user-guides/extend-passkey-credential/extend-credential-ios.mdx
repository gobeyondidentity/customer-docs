## Overview

This article is for users who have already registered a Beyond Identity passkey on their device and want to extend it to an iOS device, such as an iPhone.

By completing this process, you'll create a backup of your passkey, making it possible to restore it if it's ever deleted from the original device.

<br/>
---

### What You'll Learm
This article will walk you through the process of adding or extending your passkey credential to a trusted iOS device. 


<br/>
---

### Steps

1. Download the **Beyond Identity Authenticator** to your iOS device. You can do this by navigating to the App Store and search for **Beyond Identity**. You can also navigate to https://app.byndid.com/downloads, then click Download for iOS, which will take you to the Apple App Store.
