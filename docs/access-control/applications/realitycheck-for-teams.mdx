## Overview

RealityCheck for Teams is a Microsoft Teams plug-in for Beyond Identity customers to secure their Microsoft Teams meetings with our zero-trust, phish-resistant MFA solution. Protect your Microsoft Teams meetings from generative AI and deepfakes by strongly verifying users and devices on the call.

Check out our [solutions page](https://www.beyondidentity.com/solutions/ai-deception-elimination) or [integrations guide](https://support.beyondidentity.com/hc/en-us/articles/30574119964183-RealityCheck-for-Teams-Integration-Guide) for more information.

## Prerequisites

- Beyond Identity tenant
- Microsoft Teams License
- Apps for Teams enabled

**NOTE:** You MUST be an existing Beyond Identity customer to have RealityCheck enabled.

### Admin Installation

1. In your Admin Console, navigate to the **Access Control** section and select **Applications**.
2. Click **Browse Applications** and select **Generic OIDC**.
3. Click **Add OIDC**.
4. Input the following values:
    - **Display Name:** RealityCheck for Teams
    - **Redirect URI:** `https://realitycheck-teams.beyondidentity.com/auth/callback`
    - **Show Tile**: Disable show tile
5. Click **Save Changes**.
6. Note the following values for use later:
    - **Client ID**
    - **Client Secret**
    - **Issuer**
7. Assign the application to a group of users.
8. Submit a [support ticket](https://support.beyondidentity.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) with the following values:
    - **Subject Line**: RealityCheck for Teams intake
    - **Client ID** from step 6
    - **Client Secret** from step 6
    - **Issuer** from step 6
    - **Company Identifier**: a unique identifier for your organization
       - We **strongly recommend** your email domain as the company identifier. For example, if your email address is first.last@beyondidentity.com, your company identifier should be `beyondidentity`.
    - **Company Name**: a name for your organization
9. Our support team will follow up on next steps.

### End-user Installation

1. In any team, channel, or meeting, click **Apps**.
2. Search for "RealityCheck" and click **Install**.
3. RealityCheck should be available and installed if the steps above were followed by your admin.
4. If the App is not installed, you can always search "RealityCheck" in the app marketplace in any channel, chat, or meeting.
