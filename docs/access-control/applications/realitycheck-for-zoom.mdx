## Overview

RealityCheck for Zoom is a Zoom plug-in for Beyond Identity customers to secure their Zoom meetings with our zero-trust, phish-resistant MFA solution. Protect your Zoom meetings from generative AI and deepfakes by strongly verifying users and devices on the call.

Check out our [solutions page](https://www.beyondidentity.com/solutions/ai-deception-elimination) or [integrations guide](https://support.beyondidentity.com/hc/en-us/articles/24038107991191-RealityCheck-for-Zoom-Integration-Guide) for more information.

## Prerequisites

- Beyond Identity tenant
- Zoom License

**NOTE:** You MUST be an existing Beyond Identity customer to have RealityCheck enabled.

### Admin Installation

1. In your Admin Console, navigate to the **Access Control** section and select **Applications**.
2. Click **Browse Applications** and select **Generic OIDC**.
3. Click **Add OIDC**.
4. Input the following values:
    - **Display Name:** RealityCheck for Zoom
    - **Redirect URI:** `https://realitycheck.beyondidentity.com/auth/callback`
    - **Show Tile**: Disable show tile
5. Click **Save Changes**.
6. Note the following values for use later:
    - **Client ID**
    - **Client Secret**
    - **Issuer**
7. Assign the application to a group of users.
8. Submit a [support ticket](https://support.beyondidentity.com/hc/en-us/requests/new) with the following values:
    - **Subject Line**: RealityCheck for Zoom intake
    - **Client ID** from step 6
    - **Client Secret** from step 6
    - **Issuer** from step 6
    - **Company Identifier**: a unique identifier for your organization
       - We **strongly recommend** your Zoom domain as the company identifier. For example, if your Zoom subdomain is `beyondidentity.zoom.us`, your company identifier should be `beyondidentity`.
9. Our support team will follow up on next steps.

### End-user Installation

As an admin, you can install the RealityCheck app for your users.

1. Go to the [RealityCheck for Zoom app](https://marketplace.zoom.us/apps/ZgT5jp7xTfyqGks_h8M-Jg) on the Zoom App Marketplace.
2. Click **Install**.
3. Click **Install for Others**.
4. Select the users or groups you want to install the app for.
5. Click **Install**.
