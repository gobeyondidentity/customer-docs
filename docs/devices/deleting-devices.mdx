#Deleting a device
**Note:** This will not delete the passkey from the authenticator that allows users to securely sign into corporate applications without passwords.
<ol>
 <li>Click the Edit icon under the Actions column on the Devices page.
 ![Edit icon]("../images/devices/Actions col.png")
</li>
<li> Click **Delete Device**. A message displays that the device was successfully deleted and the device is removed from the Device table.
 ![Edit Device dialog]("../images/devices/Edit device.png")
</li>
</ol>
